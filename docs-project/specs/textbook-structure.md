# Textbook Structure Spec

## 1. Audience

- Beginner to intermediate students:
  - AI, Robotics, Physical AI, Humanoids.

## 2. Major Sections

1. **Foundations**
   - How to use this book
   - Why physical AI matters
   - Why humanoids
   - Digital to embodied intelligence
   - Data from the real world
   - Robot sensors and perception
   - Robot control systems
   - Actuation and locomotion
   - Humanoid design & kinematics

2. **Humanoid Robotics Basics**
   - Reference frames
   - Balance & stability
   - Walking & locomotion basics
   - Hands / grippers / manipulation

3. **AI for Humanoids**
   - Perception models
   - Policy learning
   - Planning & control
   - Safety basics

4. **Physical AI Applications**
   - Education
   - Industry
   - Service/healthcare robots
   - Everyday embodied assistants

(We can adjust with teacher instructions.)
